SEO AUDIT REPORT: ResurgentSports.com

Resurgent Sports Rehab | Sports Physical Therapy | Fairfax & Chantilly, VA
Audit Date: March 16, 2026

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Resurgent Sports Rehab has a strong clinical brand (128 Google reviews at 5.0 stars, 8 DPTs with elite credentials) and a well-structured Squarespace site with sport-specific, condition-specific, and age-group pages. However, significant on-page SEO gaps — missing meta descriptions site-wide, inconsistent heading hierarchy, under-optimized schema markup, and zero blog content — are limiting organic visibility. The site does NOT appear on page 1 for the primary keyword “sports physical therapy fairfax va” but DOES rank #1 for broader “sports PT northern virginia.” Addressing the critical items below could substantially increase organic traffic within 3-6 months.

2. E-E-A-T ASSESSMENT

Experience: 8/10

Evidence: Team page shows 8 DPTs with real clinical photos. Sport-specific pages demonstrate hands-on experience with athletes. Events page shows active community engagement (run clinics, injury screens, community events). Patient action photos (agility ladder, running analysis, sled pull) demonstrate real clinical work.

Gaps: No patient testimonials or case studies on the website (reviews are on Google only). No blog content documenting clinical experience or patient outcomes. No video content showcasing treatments or patient journeys.

Expertise: 8/10

Evidence: All 8 providers hold DPT degrees. Advanced certifications: SCS, OCS, ATC, CSCS. Specialized services: running analysis, sport-specific rehab, Precision Athlete Program.

Gaps: Credentials not prominently featured on service pages. No published research, articles, or educational content demonstrating expertise. No "About Our Credentials" explainer for patients.

Authoritativeness: 5/10

Evidence: 128 Google reviews at perfect 5.0 stars. Active social media. Community event partnerships (Vienna Run Club, Legend Wrestling, NOVA Running Club).

Gaps: No blog content for topical authority signals. No backlinks from authoritative health/sports publications detected. No media mentions or press coverage visible. Domain authority likely low due to limited backlink profile.

Trustworthiness: 7/10

Evidence: SSL/HTTPS with HSTS active. Transparent pricing on FAQ page ($220 eval, $175-190 follow-ups). Real team photos with credentials. Physical addresses for both locations.

Gaps: No privacy policy link detected. No patient testimonials on-site. No insurance/payment information page. No "What to Expect" first-visit page.

Overall E-E-A-T Score: 7/10
